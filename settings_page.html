Add Email Connection

  1. Go to Google Account Security.
  2. Ensure 2-Step Verification is enabled on your account.
  3. Go to App Passwords.
  4. Select app: Mail, select device: Other (Custom name), type "SENTINEL".
  5. Click Generate. Copy the 16-character password and paste it above.

Note: IMAP must be enabled in Gmail Settings → Forwarding and POP/IMAP.

  1. Go to Microsoft Account Security.
  2. Click Advanced security options.
  3. Under App passwords, click Create a new app password.
  4. Copy the generated password and paste it above.

For Microsoft 365 / Exchange, you may need admin-generated app passwords or OAuth. IMAP host is typically outlook.office365.com, port 993.
